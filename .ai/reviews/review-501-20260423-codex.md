## Findings

### Minor: Stale provenance after reopen (fixed)

**Issue**: After `task reopen`, the old `closed_by`/`closed_at` values are preserved as audit trail. If someone then raw-mutates the task back to `closed` without `governed_by`, `hasGovernedProvenance()` incorrectly accepted the stale `closed_by`/`closed_at` as valid provenance.

**Fix**: `hasGovernedProvenance()` now checks if `reopened_at` exists and is after `closed_at`. If so, the old provenance markers are considered stale and `governed_by` is required.

**Test added**: `stale closed_by/closed_at after reopen does not count as valid provenance` in `task-reopen.test.ts`.
