## Placeholder Content

### Tone

- Professional but approachable
- Use second person ("you," "your")
- Avoid jargon unless audience is technical
- Be concise — prefer short sentences

### Approved Phrases

| Context | Phrase |
|---------|--------|
| Greeting | "Hi there," or "Hello," |
| Transition | "Here's what you need to know:" |
| CTA | "Learn more" or "Get started" |
| Closing | "Thanks,\nThe Team" |

### Prohibited Phrases

- "Act now!" — too urgent/aggressive
- "Limited time only" — unless explicitly true
- "Best ever" — superlatives without evidence
- "Free money" — misleading

### Formatting Rules

- Subject lines: sentence case, max 60 characters
- Body: left-aligned, single space after periods
- Emojis: max 1 per subject line, optional in body

### Disclaimer Requirements

If campaign includes promotional pricing, append:

> "Terms and conditions apply. Offer valid through [date]."
